Heuristic Scoring Algorithm

Heuristic scoring is an intelligent code-grabbing technique. Instead of relying on strict regular expressions, it scans the email content for alphanumeric tokens (length 4–8) and weights them based on formatting and proximity to verification keywords.

This algorithm can be customized in the backend codebase at: app/Services/CodeExtractor.php Under the extractHeuristic() method, you can adjust:

  • Verification Keywords: Adjust keywords like 'code', 'pin', 'otp', etc.
  • Score Weights: Fine-tune numeric vs. alphanumeric length points.
  • Proximity Distance: Control the score bonus depending on distance from keywords.
